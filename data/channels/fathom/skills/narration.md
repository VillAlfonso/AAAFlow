# narration — fern study (voice DESIGN, never a clone)

Measured: wpm speaking 191-216 (bc3b 213, 8e1b 194, 7c7c 195, 815f 216,
da58 191). Pauses 4.3-6.9 per minute — fern BREATHES (Serious History was
1.5-2.2; do not import that rush here). Speech share 70-83%: silence under
music is part of the delivery.

## Voice design for our channel

- Base: calm male register, mid-low, minimal vibrato; precision over
  warmth; a listener should trust it like a good newsreader.
- Pace: 195-210 wpm speaking, with REAL pauses: a beat after reveals, a
  longer beat before chapter turns. Target 4-7 pauses/min.
- Tone arc: begin measured and scene-setting, tighten slightly during the
  chase/escalation, settle to quiet and thoughtful for the ending
  (falling cadence, standing guardrails apply: no ad-read, no hype, no
  rising final intonation).
- Dry wit lines are read STRAIGHT — the joke is in the words, the voice
  never winks.
- Quotes/dialogue: a slight shift in cadence, never an impression or
  character voice.
- Hedging lines ("we could not corroborate...") read slower and plainly —
  they are trust moments, not asides.
