## Rules

1. **Hold when it matters.** The signature is VARIANCE: 2-3 s cutting in
   exposition, then a 20-55 s uncut hold on archival footage, a reenacted
   call, or a map move (8e1b longest 41 s at 5:12; 7c7c 55 s at 4:38).
   Never metronome cuts.
2. **Fade-through-dark = chapter break.** Use a fade only when the story
   jumps time or location (roughly every 8-12 shots); everything else is a
   hard cut. No flashy transitions: no smash, no glitch, no whip.
3. **Leave air.** 17-30% of the runtime has no narration: montage beats
   under music, held documents, breathing room after reveals. Do not wall-
   to-wall the voice (our previous builds were 94%+ speech share).
4. **Cuts land on information, not on rhythm**: a new shot appears when the
   narration introduces a new noun/place/actor, not on a beat grid.
5. SFX: fern's sound design is ambient + music-led; per the standing rule
   stingers stay OFF; diegetic sound only when the footage itself implies
   it and only deliberately per scene.
